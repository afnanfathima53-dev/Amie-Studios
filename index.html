The Studio Story

Meet the Artist!

I am a school student who finds magic in the small details. While my days are filled with classes and projects, I dedicate my weekends and holidays to Amie Studios. As a part-time artist, I kindly ask for your patience with shipping and responses—every piece is worth the wait!

Our Unique Style

We specialize in "Animal Minis"—where nature meets imagination. Whether it's an Avofrog or a Cherribara, our style blends organic textures with cute, expressive characters. You won't find these designs anywhere else; they are exclusive, handcrafted originals.

Eco-Friendly Hearts

Born in the forest, we strive to protect it. Our miniatures are 100% plastic-free. We use sustainable materials for our clay and ship every order in recyclable, earth-conscious packaging. Art shouldn't cost the Earth.

The "Amie" Bond

In French, Amie means "friend." We don't just sell products; we create tiny companions. Because of the intensive handmade process, we don't offer refunds, but we guarantee that every "friend" is checked for quality before leaving the studio.

The Medium

Every mini is sculpted from homemade cold porcelain clay. It air-dries for 48 hours to reach a sturdy, ceramic-like finish that is incredibly lightweight. A high-gloss protective glaze is then applied to keep your friend vibrant for years.

The Vision

Amie Studios is a reminder to slow down and appreciate the handcrafted moments. From our forest studio in India to your home, we bring you small treasures that celebrate the beauty of handmade art.

Behind the Scenes

01. Sculpting

Mixing our signature cold porcelain clay and hand-sculpting each tiny character with precision tools.

02. Curing

The patience phase. Minis air-dry for two full days to achieve a sturdy, ceramic-like finish without the heat.

03. Glazing

Adding the final shine. A protective high-gloss glaze preserves the colors and adds a touch of magic.
