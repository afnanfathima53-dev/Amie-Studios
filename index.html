The Artist

Creator: Afnan

STEM student by day and miniature artist by night. I create handcrafted worlds from my forest studio, using homemade cold porcelain clay to bring tiny joy to your life.

Our Philosophy

Slow-made art with a soul. Every piece is unique, air-dried for 48 hours to ensure lightweight durability, and finished with a protective glaze.

Behind the Scenes

01. Sculpting

Mixing our signature cold porcelain clay and hand-sculpting each tiny character with precision tools.

02. Curing

The patience phase. Minis air-dry for two full days to achieve a sturdy, ceramic-like finish without the heat.

03. Glazing

Adding the final shine. A protective high-gloss glaze preserves the colors and adds a touch of magic.

🛡️ Care Guide

Your miniatures are handcrafted with cold porcelain. To keep them happy:

  • Keep Dry: Cold porcelain is water-sensitive. Keep away from liquids.
  • Soft Cleaning: Use a dry, soft brush to remove dust. Do not scrub.
  • Handle with Love: While sturdy, they are tiny art pieces, not toys.
